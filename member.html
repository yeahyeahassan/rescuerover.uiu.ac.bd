About URRT

The UIU Rescue Rover Team (URRT) is a group of passionate students dedicated to designing and building advanced rescue rovers for disaster response and emergency operations. Our mission is to leverage engineering innovation and autonomous technologies to assist in search, Rescue, and Life-saving missions.
