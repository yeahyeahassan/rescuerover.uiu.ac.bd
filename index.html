Our Achievements

Celebrating milestones that define our journey in space robotics

Mars Rover Team

2nd Runner-Up Position

BEAR Summit and National Semiconductor Symposium 2025

At the BEAR Summit 2025, the UIU Mars Rover Team (UMRT) achieved remarkable success, securing the 2nd Runner-Up position. United International University (UIU) proudly showcased its cutting-edge innovation at the BEAR Summit and National Semiconductor Symposium 2025, where the UIU Mars Rover Team earned the title of 2nd Runners-Up in the Robotics Demonstration segment. Their rover, designed and built by students of UIU’s robotics research group, stood out for its advanced engineering, problem-solving capabilities, and real-world applicability in planetary exploration. This achievement highlights UIU’s growing contribution to robotics and deep-tech research

Mars Rover Team

3rd Place at ARC 2025

Best Science & Autonomous Driving System Awards

At the Anatolian Rover Challenge (ARC) 2025 held in Turkey, the UIU Mars Rover Team (UMRT) proudly secured 3rd place overall, along with two prestigious recognitions: the Best Science Award and the Best Autonomous Driving and Control System Award. The team's latest rover, AXIOS, showcased cutting-edge AI-driven navigation, an advanced robotic arm, and powerful scientific analysis tools, proving its ability to handle the toughest terrains and complex mission tasks. These achievements mark a major milestone for UMRT, highlighting their commitment to innovation and excellence in space robotics.

Mars Rover Team

3rd Place Globally at ARC 2023

National & International Coverage

In the Anatolian Rover Challenge (ARC) 2023, the UIU Mars Rover Team (UMRT) from United International University (UIU) achieved an impressive result, securing 3rd place overall out of 38 teams from 15 countries. This achievement highlights the team's continuous growth and technical excellence in the field of planetary exploration. Their rover, TELOS, demonstrated exceptional performance in key missions, including autonomous navigation, scientific exploration, and extreme terrain traversal. UMRT's success in ARC 2023 further solidifies their reputation as one of the top competitors in international rover challenges.

About URRT

The UIU Rescue Rover Team (URRT) is a group of passionate students dedicated to designing and building advanced rescue rovers for disaster response and emergency operations. Our mission is to leverage engineering innovation and autonomous technologies to assist in search, Rescue, and Life-saving missions, pushing the boundaries of robotics for humanitarian impact.
